### 3.2 Smoothing-Aware Projection and Intervention

$$

where:

- $\phi$ is a teleological vector field acting as a targeted deformation force,

$$

- In high-relevance zones, smoothing is minimal â†’ belief shifts rapidly.
- In low-relevance zones, deformation is resisted â†’ the field remains stable.
